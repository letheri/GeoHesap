Features of NEXTgen GeoProcessor

We created a next generation calculator for your geodesic calculations.
Here is what makes it Next Generation:

High Precision

There are 7 geodesic calculations done at high precision. These functions are hand picked to be suitable to the most of yout needs.

Upload Coordinate Data

Our tool is the most conveniant of all the other calculators with a similar functions. You can upload unlimited amount of data to the calculator! (limited by your own device)

Download Results

No more copy pasting your results from calculators! That remains in the past now.
Out tool can generate a file to download all the results in one go!
